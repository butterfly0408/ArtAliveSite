About Data Collection

Type of Data Collected

On this site, we collect:

  • Full names
  • Cell phone numbers
  • Email addresses
  • Date of birth
  • Id number
  • Copy of id
  • Signature

How Data is Collected

The data collected as stated above are collected through forms the user fills out.

Why Data is Collected

Contact Form:

Through the contact form, we provide users with an avenue for contacting Art Alive directly on the site, data collected here (name and email required, phone number optional) is used solely for the purpose of responding to the communication sent to us by the form. Alternatives to this form are provided on the website in the form of contact emails and phone numbers.

Subscribe Form:

The subscribe form gives users the opportunity to submit their name and email address to Art Alive Art School in order to receive communication as selected by the user. This data is used for that purpose only.

Join Form:

The purpose of the join form is for the user to apply to classes at Art Alive Art School directly from the site. Data collected in this form will be used to process the user to join Art Alive Art School as a student. An alternative to this form is provided on the site in terms of a downloadable PDF of the form that can be filled in by the user and emailed to the provided address.

What Happens to Collected Data

Subscribe Form:

Data collected by this form is stored in a database for the purpose of emailing the user for those services to which they subscribed. An unsubscribe process is provided to the user should they wish to no longer receive emails, effectively removing all data from any database.

Join Form:

Data collected in this form is saved in a PDF file by Art Alive Art School for out records for as long as the user is a member of Art Alive Art School. Once the student leaves Art Alive Art School, all data relating to the student will be destroyed.
